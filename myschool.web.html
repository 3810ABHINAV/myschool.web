Delhi Public School is a school located in Bopal, Ahmedabad, India. School was established in 1995. Delhi Public School Bopal is English medium School affiliated with CBSE. DPS Bopal is Part of CALOREX and the present principal is Mr. Surender Pal Sacdeva
